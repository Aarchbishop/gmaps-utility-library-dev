DragZoom Back Button Simple Example

This is the minimum change to the DragZoom Control call with the single parameter "backButtonEnabled" used:


  var otherOpts = {backButtonEnabled: true};
  map.addControl(new DragZoomControl({}, otherOpts, {}));


Use the DragZoom Button ("zoom...") to initiate a DragZoom. The DragZoom Back Button ("zoom back") will appear when the DragZoom is complete. You can do a sequence of DragZooms to drill down on an area of interest. The DragZoom Back Button will take you back sequentially.
