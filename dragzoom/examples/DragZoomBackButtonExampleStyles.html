DragZoom Back Button Example with button styles and parameters

Illustrating the the customizing of the DragZoom Button and the DragZoom Back Button using styles. The Textual Zoom Control buttons are also included to illustrate a series of matching buttons for different types of zooming. Here are the styles and parameters used (otherOpts params):

var otherOpts = {
  buttonStartingStyle: { display:"block", color:"black", background:"white",
    textAlign:"center", font:"Verdana 12px bold", width:"7em", cursor:"pointer",
    paddingBottom:"1px", border:"1px solid gray"},
  buttonHTML: "Drag Zoom",
  buttonZoomingHTML: "Drag a region on the map (click here to reset)",
  buttonZoomingStyle: {background:"yellow"},
  backButtonHTML: "Drag Zoom Back",
  backButtonStyle: {display:"none", marginTop:"3px", background:"#FFFFC8"},
  backButtonEnabled: true,
  overlayRemoveTime: 1500}
